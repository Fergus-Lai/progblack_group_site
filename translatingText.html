Machine learning is being used to translate lost languages

Reserchers at MIT CSAIL develop a system that helps linguists deciper lost languages

Adrian Lea
November 1 2022

Researchers at MIT CSAIL have come up with a machine learning system that will help linguists translate texts that have been lost to time. Lost texts are texts that linguists are unable to translate due to lost knowledge and history that would of helped linguists understand the relationships between the chararcters.
